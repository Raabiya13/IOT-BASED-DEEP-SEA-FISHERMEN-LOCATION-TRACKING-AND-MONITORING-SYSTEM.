⚙️ Connection Description – Deep Sea Fisherman Tracking System.

🧠 Microcontroller Used: Arduino Uno.

🔋 Common Ground: All modules share a common GND connection.

🔘 Push Button Connection

________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

|Component-----------|--------Arduino Pin---------|Description------------------------------|
|--------------------|----------------------------|-----------------------------------------|
|Button Terminal 1   |        D2	          |Input pin to detect manual SOS trigger   |
|Button Terminal 2   |        GND		  |Connected to ground through breadboard   |
|--------------------|----------------------------|-----------------------------------------|

🧭 MPU6050 (Motion Sensor) Connection

|MPU6050 Pin|-- Arduino Pin-|---Description---------|
|VCC	    |   5V          |        Power supply   |
|GND	    |   GND         |        Ground         |
|SDA	    |   A4          |        I2C Data line  |
|SCL	    |   A5          |        I2C Clock line |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------------|

________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

📡 GPS Module (NEO-6M) Connection

|GPS Pin----|-- Arduino Pin-|--------Description----------------------------------|
|VCC	    |   5V          |        Power supply                                 |
|GND	    |   GND         |        Common Ground                                |
|TX	    |   D9          |        Transmit data (connects to Arduino RX)       |
|RX	    |   D8          |        Receive data (connects to Arduino TX)        |
|-----------|---------------|-----------------------------------------------------|

________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

📶 GSM Module (SIM800L) Connection

|GSM Pin----|-- Arduino Pin--------|--------Description----------------------------------|
|VCC	    |   5V(via Breadboard  |        Power supply (ensure sufficient current)     |
|RST	    |   -                  |        (Optional–can be left unconnected/connected) |
|RXD	    |   D5                 |        Arduino TX pin (to send data to GSM)         |
|TXD	    |   D4                 |        Arduino RX pin (to receive data from GSM)    |
|GND	    |   GND(common)        |        Ground                                       |
|-----------|----------------------|-----------------------------------------------------|

________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________________

⚡ Power Supply Notes
========================
Use an external 5V power supply or a breadboard power module to power GSM and GPS modules (as SIM800L draws higher current during transmission).
   Ensure all grounds (Arduino, GSM, GPS, MPU6050, and button) are connected together.
